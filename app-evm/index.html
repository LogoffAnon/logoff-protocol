How It Works

Three steps to disappear

Powered by Groth16 zkSNARKs and LogOff commitment trees. Simple for you, impossible for trackers.

01

Deposit

Deposit ETH into the shielded pool. A cryptographic commitment is generated and anchored to the on-chain commitment tree. Your deposit becomes one among many.

02

Shield

Your funds rest in the privacy pool. A zero-knowledge proof is generated client-side, proving ownership without revealing which deposit belongs to you.

03

Withdraw

Withdraw to any fresh address via the relayer network. The proof is verified on-chain with complete sender-recipient unlinkability. No link. No trace.

Protocol

Built for peace of mind

Military-grade privacy, wrapped in simplicity.

Groth16 Proofs

On-chain proof verification using BN254 elliptic curve. Proves note ownership without revealing any details.

LogOff Commitment Tree

SNARK-optimized incremental hash tree with depth 20, supporting over 1 million deposits.

Relayer Network

Decoupled transaction submission provides complete sender-recipient unlinkability.

Nullifier Registry

On-chain nullifier tracking prevents double-spending while maintaining zero-knowledge.

Ethereum Native

Secured by Ethereum consensus. Built with Foundry and battle-tested OpenZeppelin libraries.

Non-Custodial

No trusted third party. No KYC. No logs. Your keys, your privacy, your ETH.
